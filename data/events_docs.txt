Wedding events usually require venue booking, catering, decoration, music, and photography.

Birthday parties often focus on theme-based decorations, games, cake, and entertainment.

Corporate events require professional venues, AV setup, formal catering, and branding.

Budget optimization tips:
- Choose seasonal venues
- Limit guest list
- Use digital invitations

Popular themes:
- Wedding: Royal, Beach, Minimalist
- Birthday: Cartoon, Neon, Retro
- Corporate: Formal, Product Launch, Tech Summit
